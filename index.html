Frequently Asked Questions

How much does windshield chip repair cost?

We offer flat-rate windshield chip repair starting at $80 — whether you have one chip or a few. If the damage is too extensive to repair safely, we'll recommend a replacement. Call (980) 242-8339 for a free quote.

Do you offer mobile auto glass service?

Yes. We provide mobile windshield repair and replacement throughout Iredell County, including Mooresville and Statesville. We come to your home or workplace, or you can visit our shop at 1892 Charlotte Hwy, Unit 1, Mooresville.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes — we accept all major insurance companies and handle the claim paperwork for you. With comprehensive coverage, windshield replacement is often little or no out-of-pocket cost.

Do I need ADAS calibration after a windshield replacement?

If your vehicle has Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (lane-keep assist, automatic emergency braking, etc.), the camera mounts to the windshield and must be recalibrated after replacement. We perform ADAS recalibration in-house.

Should I repair or replace my windshield?

Small chips and short cracks outside the driver's line of sight can usually be repaired. Larger cracks, damage in the driver's view, or edge damage typically require replacement. Our AGSC-certified techs will advise during your free quote.

What areas do you serve?

Mooresville, Statesville, Lexington, and the surrounding Iredell County, NC area — both in-shop and mobile.
